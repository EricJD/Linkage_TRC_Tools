Doug, currently Managing Director of Ocean Recovery Alliance, also co-founded [[Project Kaisei]] in 2009, which led a science expedition to the [[North Pacific Gyre]] with [[Scripps Institution of Oceanography]].{{cite web |url=http://www.oceanrecov.org/about/steering-committee.html |title= Ocean Recovery Alliance Board of Directors}}The [[United Nations Environment Programme]] (UNEP) listed [[plastic pollution]] in the ocean as the first of three main focal points in their 2011 UNEP Yearbook,{{cite web |url=http://www.unep.org/yearbook/2011/ |title= 2011 UNEP Yearbook}} indicating the necessity of addressing how we use and dispose of plastics. One of the causes of plastic pollution is that so many of our disposable, single-use products, are made with a permanent material - plastic.{{cite web |url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/2017/04/plastic-straws-ocean-trash-environment/ |title= Straw Wars: The Fight to Rid the Oceans of Discarded Plastic}}  With the growth of our disposable lifestyles has come a growing burden on communities and governments whose waste management and recycling infrastructures have not been able to keep pace with our consumption patterns and waste generation.{{cite web |url=http://tedxtalks.ted.com/video/TEDxWanChai-Douglas-Woodring-Oc |title= Tedx WanChai - Douglas Woodring - Ocean Recovery Alliance}} Ocean Recovery Alliance is working on ways to combat this issue through the following projects:==Projects==The group is an umbrella organization which operates and organizes a variety of sub-projects related to the reduction of plastic pollution, ocean conservation, and general awareness about the value of our waters.{{cite web|title=Oceanic Global Fundation desembarca en Ibiza|url=http://www.elmundo.es/baleares/2017/04/29/59044ff5ca4741740c8b45e9.html|website=EL MUNDO|date=29 April 2017|accessdate=3 May 2017}}{{cite web|title=La ONG Oceanic Global Foundation se presentará en sociedad en Ibiza|url=http://www.diariodeibiza.es/pitiuses-balears/2017/04/28/ong-oceanic-global-foundation-presentara/912228.html|website=DIARIO de IBIZA|date=28 April 2017|accessdate=3 May 2017}} Two of the projects within the group were announced at the Clinton Global Initiative (CGI) in 2010, and are focused on innovative prevention programs for [[plastic waste]] reduction.  This includes the [[Plastic Disclosure Project]],{{cite web|url=http://www.hamiltonadvisorsltd.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/Spring_2012_Shrinking_Your_Plastic_Footprint.pdf |title=Stanford Social Innovation Review |work = Leland Stanford Jr. University |date = Spring 2012}} and the [http://www.oceanrecov.org/global-ocean-alert-system/critical-need.html Global Alert] platform.
